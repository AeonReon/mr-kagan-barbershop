About Us

The Barbershop Coleraine Keeps Coming Back To

Mr Kagan Barbershop has been a Coleraine institution for over seven years. What started as a passion for sharp cuts and proper barbering has grown into one of the most trusted names on the North Coast — with nearly 200 five-star reviews to prove it.

The team — including Nuri Alco, Sheldon, and Joel Duncan — are known for their skill, their friendliness, and a level of service that keeps customers driving from Portrush, Portstewart, Ballymoney, Ballycastle and beyond. Turkish-style hot towel shaves, precision fades, and a cup of tea while you wait. That's the Mr Kagan way.
